The Conversation title sequence

Video

The goal of this project was to reimagine the title sequence of the Francis Ford Coppola movie "The Conversation".
This movie takes place in the '70s, right after the 30-year post-war boom. At this time, the USA is targeted by a spy case under the Nixon government.
Gradually, the citizen is realizing that everything is far from looking good and the government isn't what it pretends to be. That's what I wanted to show in this title sequence.
