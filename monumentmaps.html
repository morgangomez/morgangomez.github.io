MonuMent Maps

Design of three distinct typographic “M” symbols, each defined by specific characteristics, and integrated into custom 3D environments combining modeling and texturing to reflect their identity.
The project aimed to create natural 3D environments entirely from scratch, providing an opportunity to further deepen and refine a wide range of technical skills.

Type & signs design: Louise Jardin
3D environments & sign sculpting: Morgan Gomez

This project is part of an ongoing independent R&D process.

Arctic

Volcano

Creating this lava landscape was a great challenge, as it required extensive use of displacement maps. I had to carefully coordinate lighting with ground and rock materials to achieve a credible and realistic environment.

Meadows

Meadows was the most interesting environment to create, as it involved weight painting and distributing assets across a given surface. This is what enables the creation of realistic natural landscapes.
