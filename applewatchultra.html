Apple Watch Ultra

This work originated from hands-on exploration of advanced 3D lighting techniques applied to premium tech products. The approach is versatile and can extend to a wide range of items, including phones, audio gear, timepieces, and more. It examines how nuanced lighting choices influence visual perception, adding a sense of depth and bringing out material qualities within a carefully controlled 3D setting.

This project is part of an ongoing independent R&D process.
