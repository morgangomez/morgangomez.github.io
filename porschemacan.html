Porsche
Macan Turbo

This project consists of a short animation and a series of visuals developed around the Porsche Macan Turbo, conceived as a focused study on automotive lighting and vehicle animation.

Multiple lighting setups were explored to accurately define the car’s surfaces, proportions and reflections, while animation tests were conducted to refine motion and presence. Rain effects were generated directly in Blender, and post-production work in After Effects was used to enhance atmosphere, contrast and realism in the rain-driven scenes.

This project is part of an ongoing independent R&D process.

Early explorations

These initial visuals stem from preliminary research focused on an engine-like object, used as a test subject for lighting, material response and surface definition.

This exploratory phase ultimately lead to a broader creative direction. Rather than limiting the study to isolated engine renders, the research naturally evolved into a full-vehicle project, allowing for a more comprehensive and compelling exploration of lighting, motion and atmosphere within an automotive context.
