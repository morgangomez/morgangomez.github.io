Ropes rigid body simulations

Self-directed study of rigid body simulations in Cinema 4D, combined with cross-software lighting and rendering in Blender. This project allowed me to explore dynamics, collisions, and material behavior while developing a robust workflow between tools, refining both technical skills and creative control over the final visuals.
