Premières Avant-Scènes

Premières Avant-Scènes is a classical music festival gathering new generation of music performers as well as long-time experts. With agency Les Belles Causes, we created a name, logo, and a complete brand system reflecting the festival’s ambition for openness and excellence. The event is founded and directed by Jean-Paul Scarpitta and held at the Potager du Roi in Versailles.

Agency: Les Belles Causes
Creative Director: Sébastien Faure-Llorens
