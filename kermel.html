Metaskin Design

Kermel is a leading industrial specialist in high-performance fibers engineered to resist fire, extreme heat, and electric arcs. These fibers form the backbone of advanced protective clothing for firefighters, industrial operators, law enforcement, and military personnel.

With the design agency Les Belles Causes, we designed and developed Kermel’s complete visual identity and brand environment, including its exhibition stand, reflecting the company’s expertise, innovation, and commitment to safety.

Agency: Les Belles Causes
Creative Director: Sébastien Faure-Llorens
Design Director: Guillaume Pesce
Art Director: Morgan Gomez

Mimethic

To strengthen its CSR initiatives, Kermel has launched Mimethic, a recycling program for the fibers used in protective solutions for the military, law enforcement, and industrial sectors. The agency contributed to the creation of the brand name, followed by the development of its visual identity and brand universe.
