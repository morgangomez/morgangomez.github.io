FUTUREPOP

Album covers, posters

Mini-fictive project I did to train myself at page layout on inDesign. The goal was to create a mini identity that could be available in different forms, such as tickets, flyers or vinyl sleeves.

Futurepop is a fictive music festival that promotes new electronic/pop artists every year. This year, the main artists were Hannah Diamond, Charli XCX, Slayyyter and Poppy.

Each year, I update the mixtape cover and create a few posters to promote new artists.
