ORegen

Identity, video

Following the numerous warnings issued by various environmental activists, confidential informations were made public by a group of activists.

The report is alarming. The Earth is slowly dying, and we, the humans, are the problem.

Thanks to this awareness, a global pressure group has emerged in many countries around the world. This group of activist-scientists, joined by more and more people, has led a revolution on a worldwide scale, taking control of a global state promoting an innovative and radical solution: scheduling an intentional extinction of the human race.

Creation of an utopian state and its propaganda video from scratch.
