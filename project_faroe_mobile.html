Faroe Islands

Posters

I wanted to do a set of posters that promotes the Faroe Islands to a younger audience (18-25 y.o.).

To do this, I used some well known social medias. I wanted to show what a Faroe Island photo would look like on a Twitter, Facebook or Instagram post.

I wanted the social media posts to have many likes to tell people something like "look, if you go to the Faroe Islands and post a photo of the landscape, you'll have many likes!" (basically).
