Space and its representation

Editorial, dissertation, copy strategy

Project made during my 2nd year of graphic design studies.
I had to make a dissertation and a copy strategy based on my end-of-studies project.

The dissertation is about Space and its representation : how do you represent the invisible? The unknown?
The second part is the copy strategy, and my end-of-studies project is to remake the visual identity and website of the Paris Observatory.
