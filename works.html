Locked Down

School project - Photography, 3D

This project shows how I felt like during the first lockdown in France, which lasted 3 months from march to june 2020.
I was addicted to 24/7 media channels. My state of mind was very unstable and changed every other days. That's why I made a series of photos where each picture shows a particular state of mind that I was in at the time.

This is the story of how the medias and screens locked me down, and how I broke free from my own obsession.

Paris Observatory

School project - Visual identity, webdesign, video

The Paris Observatory is the oldest one in the whole world. However, it is left out in favor of larger, more reachable structures.

It has a huge historic, scientific and educative potential that too little people are aware of. Still to this day, the Paris Observatory remains behind its competitors.

My goal here was to freshen up the Paris Observatory with a brand new visual identity, making us want to discover more about space and its secrets.

LoveID

School project - Generative design, print

What is love? Many will just say "Baby, don't hurt me". But when we think of it, Love is maybe the most complicated and blurry feeling. It is in the center of stories, songs, passions. Some will say that it's just a chemical process that goes into our brain, but the thing is : there is no real answer.

So, to push forward the research on this topic, I created LoveID, a love-analysing software made to understand yourself a bit more.
