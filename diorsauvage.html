Sauvage
Eau de Parfum

Initially conceived as part of an exploration of material, light and atmosphere, this background environment became the foundation for a new creative direction. Rather than remaining a purely exploratory piece, the asset was developed into a standalone visual study.

It found its natural expression through a fragrance visualisation for Dior Sauvage. The project reflects a strategic approach to design: creating visual assets with enough depth and versatility to transcend their original purpose and support evolving brand narratives.

This project is part of an ongoing independent R&D process.
